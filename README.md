## 📘 **Project Overview**
This project analyzes an e-commerce sales dataset containing 3,500 orders with 7 key features.  
The goal is to identify revenue trends, top-performing categories, high-profit regions, and provide actionable insights.

---

## 📦 **Dataset Details**
- Rows: 3,500  
- Columns: 7  
- No missing values  
- Realistic e-commerce data  
- Features: Order Date, Product Name, Category, Region, Quantity, Sales, Profit  

---

## 🛠 **Tools Used**
- Python  
- Pandas  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

## 📊 **Key KPIs**
- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Monthly Sales Trend  
- Sales by Region  
- Sales by Category  
- Top 10 Products  
- Profit Margin  

---

## 📈 **Visualizations**
- Monthly Sales Trend  
- Sales by Category  
- Sales by Region  
- Top 10 Products  
- Profit vs Sales Scatter Plot  

---

## 💡 **Insights**
- West region generates highest sales  
- Electronics category dominates  
- December shows peak revenue  
- Profit and revenue have positive correlation  
- Top 10 products contribute major revenue share  

---

## 📌 **Recommendations**
- Increase stock for high-demand products  
- Improve supply chain for low-profit regions  
- Launch Q4 seasonal campaigns  
- Revisit pricing for low-margin items  

---
## 📊 Python Dashboard

An interactive dashboard is created using Streamlit.

Features:
- KPI metrics (Sales, Profit, Orders)
- Monthly sales trend
- Sales by region and category
- Top 10 products
- Interactive filters
